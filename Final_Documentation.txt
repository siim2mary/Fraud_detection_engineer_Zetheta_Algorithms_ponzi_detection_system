   1.Technical Architecture Overview
    System Logic

This section describes how raw blockchain-style transaction data is transformed into an interpretable **risk assessment signal**.

### 1. Data Ingestion

* Simulates 60 days of on-chain activity using `bitconnect_test.py`.
* Generates timestamped inflow/outflow transactions to mimic investor behavior and contract payouts.
* Outputs a normalized transaction ledger used by downstream engines.

### 2. Yield Sustainability Engine

* Computes the Sustainability Ratio:

  [
  R_s = \frac{\text{Outflow}}{\text{Inflow}}
  ]

* Interprets system health as:

  * (R_s < 1): Inflows exceed outflows (temporarily sustainable)
  * (R_s \approx 1): Fragile equilibrium
  * (R_s > 1): Structurally unsustainable (Ponzi-like behavior)

* Produces a rolling time-series signal capturing yield stress.

### 3. Wealth Concentration Engine

* Measures capital distribution using the **Gini Coefficient (G)**.
* Detects increasing wealth centralization among early or dominant wallets.
* Rising (G) values indicate reduced liquidity resilience and higher collapse risk.

### 4. Risk Dynamics & Alerting Engine

* Combines (R_s) and (G) into a composite **risk score**.
* Monitors the **rate of change ((\Delta))** in risk rather than absolute thresholds.
* Triggers alerts when accelerating risk trends imply systemic instability.

### 5. Early-Warning Signal

* The alerting logic is calibrated to provide an **approximately 9-day lead time** prior to simulated collapse events.
* Emphasizes *trend-based detection* over static rule violations, aligning with real-world failure dynamics.
